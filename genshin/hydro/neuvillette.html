Pros
  • Very fun & easy playstyle
  • Pure Hp scaling damage, so doesn't compete for Atk% artifacts
  • Great solo damage without buffers
  • A lot of on-field hydro application, making him a great driver
Cons
  • Long field time (but can easily be cut short)
  • Difficult to Hypercarry as there are few Hp buffing units.
  • No off-field hydro application
  • Only heals the exact amount of self-drained Hp, so needs to be run with shield or healer
  • Fills the same role as Childe or Ayato
  • Passive encourages Rainbow teams, making double Hydro and its resonance less impactful than it should
