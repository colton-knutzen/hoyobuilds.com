Rainbow

Rainbow teams are composed of 4 separate elements, trying to capitalize on as many different reactions as they possibly can. Most Rainbow teams consist of Anemo, as it’s the only element capable of causing a chain-reaction of reactions.

Each archetype is different with its own unique playstyle. Even different teams within the same archetype will have the main reaction changing, greatly impacting the playstyle.

Disclaimer

The example teams are not intended to be the best for that specific archetype. Some of them are strong, while others are just fun teams that will struggle in the highest levels of the Abyss. Some archetypes are weaker than others, as they don’t have a strong roster to draw from. But that doesn’t make them less enjoyable to play in the overworld.

We tried restricting each team to no more than two limited 5* units.

All the archetypes have multiple lineups that work. Use these guides to help you experiment with your own teams!

Soup

Soup is the archetype of Anemo, Electro, Hydro, and Pyro. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Electro, Pyro, Hydro)
  • Electro-charged
  • Overload
  • Vaporize

The idea of Soup is to first apply Electro-charged, and then trigger Swirl, Overload, and Vaporize on top of it.

Sucrose is used for her EM buff, Anemo swirl, and grouping abilities.

Kokomi is the main on-fielder, driving Fischl and healing the team.

Gazpacho

Gazpacho is the archetype of Anemo, Electro, Hydro, and Cryo. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Electro, Cryo, Hydro)
  • Electro-charged
  • Superconduct
  • Freeze
  • Shatter

Gazpacho’s idea is to first trigger Freeze on targets, and then Superconduct, Electro-charged, and Swirl. It’s even better if a physical, Blunt dealing main DPS is used to take advantage of the Physical resistance reduction Superconduct provides.

Popcorn

Popcorn is the archetype of Anemo, Electro, Cryo, and Pyro. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Electro, Pyro, Cryo)
  • Superconduct
  • Overload
  • Melt

Since Popcorn doesn’t have an overtime reaction like Burning, Electro-charged, or Freeze, the idea is to trigger as many quick, exploding reactions as possible.

Hot Fudge Sundae

Hot Fudge Sundae is the archetype of Anemo, Hydro, Cryo, and Pyro. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Cryo, Pyro, Hydro)
  • Freeze
  • Vaporize
  • Melt
  • Shatter

Like Popcorn, Hot Fudge Sundae doesn’t have an overtime reaction. Thus, they try to trigger as many Amplification Reactions as possible.

Habanero

Habanero is the archetype of Dendro, Electro, Cryo, and Pyro. Its reactions consist of:
  • Quicken
  • Burning
  • Superconduct
  • Overload
  • Melt

Habanero aims to maintain both Quicken and Burning as overtime reactions, while using Cryo to react with both Electro and Pyro.

Forestfire

Forestfire is the archetype of Anemo, Dendro, Electro, and Pyro. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Electro, Pyro)
  • Quicken
  • Burning
  • Overload

Forestfirst aims to trigger Burning first, then Quicken. Then Anemo comes in to swirl Pyro to spread the fire and deal Overload reactions.

Tighnari’s Forestfire team is a quick-swap team with no main on-fielder.

Yogurt

Yogurt is the archetype of Anemo, Dendro, Electro, and Cryo. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Electro, Cryo)
  • Quicken
  • Superconduct

Yogurt has the least amount of reactions of all Rainbow teams because Dendro doesn’t react with Anemo or Cryo. But that makes this team good for Physical Electro characters, as they can reliably trigger both Quicken and Superconduct.

Fridge

Fridge is the archetype of Anemo, Dendro, Hydro, and Cryo. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Cryo, Hydro)
  • Bloom
  • Freeze

Like Yogurt, Fridge has few reactions since Dendro doesn’t react with Anemo or Cryo. But it’s powerful nevertheless. Fridge aims to Freeze the targets first, then trigger Bloom on the Frozen targets, with Anemo grouping and keeping enemies Frozen for as long as possible.

Campfire

Campfire is the archetype of Anemo, Dendro, Pyro, and Cryo. Its reactions consist of:
  • Swirl (Cryo, Pyro)
  • Burning
  • Melt

Campfire aims to Burn targets and then trigger Reverse Melt with Cryo. Anemo is used to Swirl the campfire onto other enemies during the chilly weather.
